Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our countertop services

We offer a comprehensive selection of premium countertop materials to suit every style and budget. Our options include granite, quartz, marble, laminate, solid surface, and butcher block. Each material has unique characteristics in terms of durability, maintenance requirements, and aesthetic appeal. During your free consultation, our design experts will help you explore samples and discuss which material best fits your lifestyle, kitchen design, and preferences. We only work with high-quality suppliers to ensure your countertops look beautiful and perform excellently for years to come.
The installation timeline varies depending on the complexity of your project, the material chosen, and any customization requirements. Most standard kitchen countertop installations can be completed in 1-3 days, with measurements typically taken during your consultation. For complex projects involving custom edges, sink cutouts, or special finishes, the process may take longer. We'll provide you with a detailed timeline during the planning phase so you know exactly what to expect. Our goal is to minimize disruption to your daily routine while ensuring every detail is executed to perfection.
Our comprehensive lifetime warranty covers defects in materials and workmanship for as long as you own your home. This includes protection against chipping, cracking, staining, and seam separation. The warranty covers both parts and labor for repairs or replacements needed due to manufacturing defects or installation issues. Normal wear and tear, damage from accidents, or improper maintenance are not covered. We also offer optional extended care packages for additional protection. When you choose CounterTop Pro, you're investing in peace of mindwe stand behind our work completely.
Maintenance requirements depend on your countertop material. Most materials can be cleaned daily with mild soap and warm water. Natural stone like granite and marble may require periodic sealing to maintain stain resistance. Quartz is non-porous and requires minimal maintenance. We provide detailed care instructions for your specific material during installation. General best practices include using cutting boards, avoiding extreme heat exposure, wiping up spills promptly, and using appropriate cleaners for your material type. Our team can recommend specific products and techniques during your consultation to help you keep your countertops looking beautiful for years.
